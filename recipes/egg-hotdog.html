Huevos Con Weenie

Description

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Hotdog
  • Black pepper
  1. Cut your hotdog into bitesize pieces.
  2. Put the hotdog pieces into a cold pan and turn on medium-high heat. Cook the hotdog until crispy.
  3. Crack your eggs into a bowl. Scramble with a fork. Pour into medium-high heat pan with cooked hotdog pieces.
  4. Keep stirring the eggs to prevent them from sticking to pan. When they are almost ready, add black pepper to your liking.
  5. Serve with tortillas and your favorite hot sauce.
