Beef Wellington

Description

This is a delectable recipe to include around Christmas time. A beautiful piece of roast, a layer of premium mushroom. and on top, beautiful pastry crust cut to form a decorative layer. It will take a few hours to cook, but it is so worth it. Be careful, I think your family will be begging you to make it every year.

  • 2 x 400g beef fillets
  • Olive oil, for frying
  • 500g ixture of wild mushrooms, cleaned
  • 1 thyme sprig, leaves only
  • 500g puff pastry
  • 8 slices of Parma ham
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten with 1 tbsp water and a pinch of salt
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
